Geekay Games:


Geekay Games is one of the main video game stores in Dubai. They have multiple locations in places such as Mall of the Emirates, Mirdif City Center, Dubai Mall and more. They have a large variety of games for all consoles and sell other equipment as well, such as headphones, but the range of other equipment is quite limited.

Virgin Megastore:


Virgin Megastore is not mainly a video game store, however, they contain a wide variety of games, while also selling various other things like electronics, music, books and more. They have a wide range of equipment such as headphones, keyboards, controllers and more. They have locations in Dubai Mall and Mall of the Emirates. Their stores are very large and have lots of items, making it useful for buying multiple things at once.
