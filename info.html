The UAE is made up of seven Emirates. These seven Emirates are: Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Ajman, Sharjah, Fujairah, Ras Al Khaimah and Umm Al Quwain. The capital city of the UAE is Abu Dhabi, but the most known city is Dubai. The current President of the United Arab Emirates, and the Ruler of Abu Dhabi is His Highness Sheikh Khalifa bin Zayed Al Nahyan. The current Vice President of the UAE and Ruler of Dubai is His Highness Sheikh Mohammed bin Rashid Al Maktoum.

The UAE is a small country, with an area of only 83,600 km2. It has a GDP (Gross Domestic Product) of 382 billion USD and a GDP per capita of 40,698 USD. The country has a low population of only 9.4 million people.

One of the main sources of income for the Governement of the UAE is oil. The UAE is a major exporter of oil. Another main source of income is tourism, espicially within Dubai. There is a VAT (Value Added Tax) on goods and services of 5%.
