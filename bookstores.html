Kinokuniya:



Kinokuniya is the largest bookstores within the UAE. However, they have only one branch in the UAE. The bookstore is in Dubai Mall, which is the largest mall in the UAE. Because of its location, it's easily accessible from most parts of the city. The bookstore has the largest range of books out of all the bookstores, and it has many different types of books. Due to these factors, it is the best bookstore in the UAE.

Borders:


Borders is another bookstore in the UAE. In comparison to Kinokuniya, Borders has multiple stores within the UAE, meaning it is very accessible. It has stores in Ibn Batuta Mall, Mall of the Emirates, Spring Souk and more. It also has a wide range of books, but not as big as Kinokuniya.

Magrudy's:


Magrudy's is a bookstore that has been in the UAE for a decades. Like Borders, they have many locations. they also offer online delivery, meaning the physical store doesn't have to be visited. On top of books, they also sell stationery, toys and more. They also have location outside of Dubai. They have locations in Jumeriah, World Trade Center, and some locations in Abu Dhabi as well.
