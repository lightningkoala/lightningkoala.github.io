The UAE is a country within the Middle East, near the Arabian Gulf. The UAE is a very diverse country, having many people from all countries, background, races and religions, and is tolerant of all kinds of people. The UAE is one of the main tourist hotspots across the world and has attractions like the Burj Khalifa, which is currently the world's tallest building, and stands at a height of 828m.

This website contains lots of useful information about the UAE, and about the various parts that make the UAE unique when compared to the rest of the world. On this website, there will be information regarding things such as the laws in the UAE, which are quite different from other countries.
